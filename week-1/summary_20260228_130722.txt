# Summary

Kroger is one of America's largest supermarket chains, operating more than 2,700 stores across 35 states under multiple brand names and employing approximately 430,000 workers with over $148 billion in 2023 revenue. The company is modernizing its operations through significant investments in automation technology, including automated fulfillment centers and AI-driven inventory management systems. However, Kroger faces mounting competition from major retailers such as Amazon Fresh, Walmart, and discount grocers like Aldi and Lidl. Additionally, the company's 2022 proposed merger with Albertsons has encountered substantial regulatory challenges.
